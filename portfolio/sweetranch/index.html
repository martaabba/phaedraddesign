Frequently Asked Questions

How long have you been bording horses?

We are in our 10th year of operating the bording facility, however, the Veteranary practice has been ongoing for the last 20 years as a separate practice.

Can you provide references?

We are more than happy to provide references and testamonials upon request.

Where do you source your feed and hay?

We try to source our feed as much as possible within the valley, however, we can provide your horse with specialty feed upon request.

Do you provide breaking for horses or training for riders?

We do not currently staff for training, as we are mostly a boarding facilty. We do, howver, have a training ring that is available for your use upon reservation at no extra cost to borders. We can also provide a list of preferred traininers upon request.

Do you host events at your location?

In general, we do not, however exceptions have been known to be made. Please inquire if interested and we can go over your options.
